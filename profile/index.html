A little bit about myself

Hello there, my name is Joshua Söhn and I'm a designer and front-end developer from Düsseldorf, Germany.

I love to craft user-friendly interfaces and build beautiful websites by using simple design and modern web-standards. Bringing content to the user without distracting him and using beautiful typography and color schemes to achieve that is my passion and my hobby.

After being self-taught for about 3 years I am now working and learning at Grey Germany. I'm also working part-time as an UX and visual designer at Lookbook.

Services

—Design

Editorial, Interface, Illustration, Brand, Art Direction, and Web, with a focus on responsive design strategy and integrated storytelling using image and text.

—Consultancy

Over the years, we’ve built a network of the best illustrators, photographers, developers, writers, and editors. If our project needs that extra touch, we know who’d be best. We love to collaborate.

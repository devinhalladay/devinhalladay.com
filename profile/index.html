A little bit about myself

My name is Devin Halladay.
I'm a graphic designer, writer, and humanist.

I craft honest visual solutions. I sincerely care about the stories with which I work and I have an ambition toward the finest of execution.

I value craftsmanship, curation, and careful research. I try to make those characteristics present in every project I work on. Building beautiful and functional designs does not just mean making something visually appealing — it means taking every pixel into consideration and giving an equal amount of care to every minute detail.

Design is my passion.

Services

—Design

Editorial, Interface, Illustration, Brand, Art Direction, and Web, with a focus on responsive design strategy and integrated storytelling using image and text.

—Consultancy

Concept, Strategy, Copy, Imagery, with a focus on finding consistent voice, appropriate tone, and overall fit.

—Community

Over the years, we’ve built a network of the best illustrators, photographers, developers, writers, and editors. If our project needs that extra touch, we know who’d be best. We love to collaborate.

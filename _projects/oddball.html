Oddball is a family-owned-and-run lifestyle and fashion company (and by family, I mean part of my family: my eldest brother and my dad). While they design the products, I have been responsible for the branding, multiple websites, and brand strategy.

To date I've worked on a ton of promotional materials, brand collateral, web design and development, and identity design. The brand is still taking shape and continuously evolving, but the future of the brand looks promising.
